Game Modification: Hold The Line
Hold The Line was a simple strategy card game. I wanted to explore the rock-paper-scissors triangle inside of a strategic card & board game. Players exchange turns placing military units in an attempt to break through their opponent's defense. Unfortunately, due to poor balance, the game goes on for significantly longer than desired or intended.
